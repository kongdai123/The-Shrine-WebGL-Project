Mouse Drag: Adjust bird orientation

Keyboard Contorls:
Space: Start Stop bird

Camera Movement:
W: move camera up the screen
S: move camera down the screen
A: strafe camera left horizontally
D: strafe camera right horizontally
Q: move camera into the screen
E: move camera out of the screen

Camera Rotation (Implements a spherical camera conrtol):
J: rotate camera right handedly with respect to y axis of screen (look left)
L: rotate camera left handedly with respect to y axis of screen (look right)
I: rotate camera right handedly with respect to x axis of screen (look up)
K: rotate camera left handedly with respect to x axis of screen (look down)
U: rotate camera left handedly with respect to z axis pointing in the screen (tilt conunter-clockwise)
O: rotate camera right handedly with z axis pointing in the screen (tilt clockwise)

Zoom settings:
N: zoom in, decrease zoom angle 
M: zoom out, increase zoom angle
